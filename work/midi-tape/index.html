midi-tape

A multi-track track recorder for MIDI

In quarantine I decided to build a multi-track tape recorder for MIDI, in the style of old TASCAM 4-track cassette recorders and the Teenage Engineering OP-1. It's web based, using Web MIDI to record and play back MIDI events. For more information, visit https://mortenson.coffee/midi-tape and give it a try yourself, and read my extensive blog post about its implementation.
